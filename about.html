About Event Finder

Event Finder is your go-to platform for discovering amazing events happening in Coimbatore. From college festivals to cultural events, we keep you updated with the latest happenings in the city.

How It Works

We gather information from various sources and automatically update our database with the latest events. Users can search for events based on their interests and location. For ticket booking, we redirect you to the official event website to ensure a secure and reliable booking experience.

Our Vision & Mission

Our mission is to make it easy for people to find and attend events that match their interests. We aim to create a seamless and enjoyable experience for our users.
